Group Policies

Starting from version 1.5.0, PDFCreator now supports group policies. The group policies are available as admx files here:

http://www.pdfforge.org/files/pdfcreator/PDFCreator_Group_Policy.zip

Currently, settings applied by a policy will still be changable by users temporarly, the related options will still be accessable.

The policies will be applied with every new start of PDFCreator though.

We are currently working on this problem.

Further information on group policies can be found at wikipedia or professional literature.
